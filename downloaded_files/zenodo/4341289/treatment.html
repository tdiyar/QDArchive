Remarks. The type locality of Bioramix reinigi is unable to confirm, type locality of Platynoscelis tibetana may be Bayan Ka La Shan, southwest of Madoi County, Qinghai, China. Based on the examination of 1500 specimens (including 2 of 6 type ones), material from Madoi County [QMD] distinctly different from Gansu [GMQ, GLG], Sichuan [SRX, SDG, SDM], Qinghai [QZH, QGH, QBM, QCX, QCC] by male aedeagus, and also Qinghai [QMX] by body size and metallic sheen of elytral surface. However, material between them presenting continuous variation. Furthermore, male aedeagus can also be varied in the same locality [QMH, QDQ, QMQ]. Therefore, we regarded all the material as B. reinigi at present morphological study.
