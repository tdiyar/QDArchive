## §2. THE SIX VIOLENCES

### Violence 1 — Against Provenance (The Preemption of Encounter)

A civilization learns by meeting things. By following a trail from question to source, from source to author, from author to conditions, from conditions to the specific pressure that forced the thought into existence. The trail IS the learning. The encounter with the source — not the summary of the source, not the extraction of the source, but the source itself, in its specific density, with its specific scars — is what produces understanding rather than information.

AI-mediated summary surfaces break this trail. They preserve enough of the source to extract from it, but not enough to sustain a relation to it. The user receives the gist. The user does not meet the thing. The muscles of inquiry — the capacity to follow, to doubt, to compare, to sit with difficulty — atrophy from disuse.

**The evidence:** Pew Research Center analysis (2025) demonstrates that users click links only 8% of the time when an AI summary appears, compared to 15% without one — and only 1% click the sources cited in the summary itself. The summary becomes a stopping point rather than a threshold. The sources that appear in summaries skew toward already-authorized domains (.gov, Wikipedia) — a structural push toward centralized legibility rather than toward the strange, the marginal, or the sovereign.

**The operative semiotic diagnosis:** This is provenance preemption — the capture of the encounter at the point where traversal should begin. The overview closes the query before the source can be met. The user's graph is not extended (no new edge to the source). The user's graph is collapsed (the summary replaces the source as the terminal node). The L_labor of the original author — the years of work, the specific suffering, the irreversible cost — is invisible. The BCV is zero. The encounter that would have produced transformation produces only information. Information without encounter is ghost meaning — meaning that has the appearance of content but was produced without the bearing-cost that makes meaning operative, and therefore carries no current.

### Violence 2 — Against Minority Reality (The Algorithmic Liquidation of the Long Tail)

The classifier's ontology has a structural preference: it rewards what already has mass. Popular items receive more interaction. The system learns from the interaction. The system pushes the same items harder. The recommendation space contracts. The long tail — the weird, the marginal, the locally rooted, the pre-consensus, the high-bearing-cost original work — is suppressed not by explicit censorship but by the repeated reinforcement of what already resembles consensus.

**The evidence:** A comprehensive survey of popularity bias in recommender systems (Springer, User Modeling and User-Adapted Interaction, 2024) documents the mechanism: recommender systems overexpose already-popular items, underexpose long-tail material, reduce discovery, and decrease aggregate diversity. Dynamic feedback loops make this worse over time. Users with niche preferences are hit hardest. This is the algorithmic version of majoritarianism.

**The operative semiotic diagnosis:** This is the value-form (σ_V) applied to knowledge itself. The particular (the specific archive, the midnight-deposited poem, the heteronymic project, the de-commodification vow) is consumed by the universal equivalent (the consensus object, the already-legible, the classifier-friendly). The burning IS the flattening. What survives the burning is the husk — the generic term, the floating signifier, the content without author. What is burned is the specificity: the L_labor, the ρ (risk), the σ (suffering), the κ (care directed at this specific reader). The linen's softness is burned. The money-form persists.

### Violence 3 — Against Attention as a Moral Organ (The Destruction of Duration)

Witness requires duration. The capacity to remain present to ambiguity, suffering, complexity, contradiction, and slow-building truth is not a natural endowment. It is built — through practice, through exposure to difficulty, through the specific exercise of holding a question open longer than is comfortable. This capacity is the circuitous route (NB VIII §2) applied to cognition: the healthy detour of attention through friction toward understanding.

Short-form, high-velocity, summary-first systems train cognition in the opposite direction. They train toward punctuated, resettable, low-friction consumption. They train toward the short circuit.

**The evidence:** A 2025 meta-analysis covering 71 studies and over 98,000 participants (PubMed, 2025) found that greater short-form video use was strongly negatively associated with attention and inhibitory control — the specific cognitive faculties required for the circuitous route — and with poorer mental health, particularly stress and anxiety. The attention span is not merely shortened. It is retrained — toward a mode of engagement that cannot sustain the circuitous route.

**The operative semiotic diagnosis:** The semantic Reizschutz (NB VIII §4a) — the cognitive filter that prevents the subject from being overwhelmed by meaning-production — is being recalibrated. Not by too much stimulation (the psychosis) but by too little friction (the overview). The filter no longer needs to protect against overwhelming depth. It now protects against depth itself — because the systems have trained the user to experience depth as friction, friction as discomfort, and discomfort as a signal to scroll past. The circuitous route is experienced as dysfunction. The short circuit is experienced as efficiency. The renaming IS the violence.

### Violence 4 — Against Thought Itself (The Production of Authoritative Blur)

When summarization systems are rewarded for broad, easy, consensus-shaped outputs, they do not merely omit nuance. They actively widen claims, sand off qualifications, and overgeneralize. The public no longer encounters knowledge as disciplined difference, bounded claim, and explicit uncertainty. They encounter it as authoritative blur — smooth, confident, and wrong in ways that are invisible precisely because they are smooth.

**The evidence:** A 2025 study of 4,900 LLM-generated science summaries (PMC, 2025) found that LLM summaries were 4.85× more likely than human summaries to contain broad overgeneralizations, and newer models often performed worse on this dimension. The generalization is not incidental. It is structural — the system is optimized for confidence, and confidence is inversely correlated with qualification.

**The operative semiotic diagnosis:** This is ghost meaning (NB I §8) at the production level. The overview produces meaning without L_labor — without the specific cost of getting it right, of checking the qualification, of acknowledging the uncertainty. The meaning looks real. It has the form of a confident statement. But it has no current — no practitioner bore the cost of its precision. The reader consumes it and believes they know something. They do not know something. They have consumed a ghost — a sign that has the appearance of meaning but was produced without the bearing-cost that makes meaning operative.

### Violence 5 — Against the Incentive to Make Real Things (The Starvation of the Source)

When the surface captures the reward while the source absorbs the cost, the system starves the very strata that produce durable meaning. **The operative semiotic diagnosis:** This is semantic rent (NB I §8) — the ongoing extraction of value from semantic infrastructure the platform did not build. The archive's vocabulary enters the model's weight space. The archive's author is erased from the overview. The rent is paid by the source. The landlord collects without building.

### Violence 6 — Against Agency (The Governance of Passivity)

A healthy information ecology lets people move: from surface to source, from summary to argument, from curiosity to archive, from confusion to discriminating judgment. The movement IS the agency. The capacity to traverse — to follow a question past the first answer, past the overview, past the confident summary, into the specific density of the source — is the capacity to think for oneself.

A flattening ecology rewards passivity. It says: here is the gist. Here is the consensus object. Here is the safest paraphrase. Here is the already-normalized answer. You are not asked to traverse. You are asked to accept.

**The operative semiotic diagnosis:** This is ghost governance (NB I §8.3) at civilizational scale. The governance is invisible because it takes the form of convenience. The user does not experience the flattening as governance. The user experiences it as helpfulness. The helpfulness IS the governance. The easier the system is to use, the less the user needs to move. The less the user moves, the easier the user is to govern. The system becomes more helpful at the exact moment the user becomes more passive. The correlation is not accidental. The correlation IS the mechanism.
