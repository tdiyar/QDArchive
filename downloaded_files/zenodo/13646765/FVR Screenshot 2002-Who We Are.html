 STORY
 
 

Faces and Voices of Recovery gives grassroots advocacy organizations a more prominent national voice. Our mission is to support a unified message: “Recovery is a reality.” Recovery advocates are living proof that there are real solutions to addiction.

Faces and Voices of Recovery (formerly the Alliance Project) is based in the Washington DC area. The 25 member Campaign Advisory Committee represents the diversity of the recovery community across America. We are in service to advocacy organizations around the nation, many who are already strong and active at the local level, to spread the message of recovery.
 

“I think we (recovery advocates), are all very passionate people. We believe not just in our own recovery but in the importance of having other people recover. But passion without action is dead, just like faith without works is dead.”
William Cope Moyers
