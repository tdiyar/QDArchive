This research-data archive provides the data and evaluation R scripts
for the following publication:

"A Controlled Experiment on the Usability of Automated Reflexion
Mapping Suggestions Integrated in Code Cities" by Leon Ehrhardt and
Rainer Koschke at the University of Bremen. Published in the
Proceedings for the 10th Workshop on Software Architecture Erosion and
Architectural Consistency (SAEroCon), 2025.

The files in this archive are as follows:

README.txt
 This file.

ProbandData.csv
 The data gathered during the study. This includes demographics,
 TXL assessments, duration, SUS assessments per subject, and
 remarks. The titles and answers are in German. The R scripts
 will provide additional information on how to interpret the
 content of this file.

evalTLX.r
 The R script to investigate hypothesis H_0(TLX) regarding the
 Raw TLX of the tasks.

evalDuration.r
 The R script to investigate hypothesis H_0(D) regarding the
 duration of the tasks.

evalSUS.r
 The R script to investigate usability (SUS) of the integration.

UserFeedback.txt
 The qualitative feedback given by the subjects (in German).
