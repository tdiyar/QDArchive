Production date: 2020/April/17

This is the derived data, presented in a publication entitled "Spatial and temporal variability of the 365-nm albedo of Venus observed by the camera on board Venus Express" (Lee et al. submitted to JGR:Planet).
All details are described in Lee et al.

Each txt file has headers, explaining the contents of columns and units.
Figure numbers shown in brackets are associated figures in Lee et al.
