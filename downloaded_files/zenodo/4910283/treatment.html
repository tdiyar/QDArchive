Distribution. Central America: Mexico, Guatemala, Belize and Honduras; South America: Colombia and Brazil (Attems 1930; Bücherl 1942; Schileyko & Minelli 1998). In Colombia N. stolli is known from the Amazonian, Orinoquia and Andean regions.

Altitudinal range. 189 to 3800 m.
