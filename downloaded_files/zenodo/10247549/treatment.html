bombyliformis Schiner, 1868: 111. Type-locality: “ Chile ”. Refs. Reed, 1888: 29 (cat.); Hunter, 1901: 150 (cat.); Kertész, 1909: 28 (cat.); Lichtwardt, 1910: 606 (Eurygastromyia); Edwards, 1930: 182.

Holotype. Male (NHMUK), Chile, Valparaíso.
